About the Author

Hussain Ahmed is a Computer Science student at Griffith College, Dublin currently pursuing a Master's in Interactive Digital Media.

He often enjoys video games with touching stories. Excluding Yoko Taro games, his favourite titles are Devil May Cry, Dark Souls, Bloodborne, Sekiro, Elden Ring and Katana Zero.

In his free time, he likes to be outside in the company of others, sharing stories and having a good cup of tea. He hopes to persue a professional career in Design, whether it is Web Design, Illustrations or Graphic Design; Whichever stimulates the challenge and creativity.
