Yoko Taro;

Making Weird Games for Weird People

Yoko Taro is a Japanese video game director and scenario writer. Best-known for his video game titles Drakengard, and its spin-offs, NieR and NieR; Automata.

Taro aims to create personal experiences through the stories he develops and translates into a medium unique only to video games, as he pushes the psychology of both the medium and the player in order to create an emotional affect in his audience.
