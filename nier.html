NieR Replicant

An ideology is a train of obedience that one follows, without even knowing why. The hero and the villian are the same entity, two sides of the same coin, only divided by the color of one's belief and acceptance of the other.

NieR Replicant chooses to focus on this idea, transforming it into a reality of its world. 3000 so years into the future, humanity rests at the edge of survival. Living a simple, primitive life, mankind is surrounded by creatures of glyphs, spells and shadows known as "Shades".

As the protagonist, it becomes a matter of duty to eliminate every single shade in order to save your sister from the Lord of all Shades.

However, even if you manage to do so, the game will end, revealing its first ending. Upon second playthrough, most things will not change, until slowly the knowledge of what you knew as Shades dawns not on the protagonist; but you the player directly.

With each fragment of truth, you - the Player - are confronted with the truth of conflict and are given the choice to conform or heal it.
