Drakengard Series

A world locked in perpetual war between humans and demi-gods, the world of Drakengard spans across ages of blood soaked tragedy, comedy and grief. The darkest entry of the series and the very beginning of Taro's tone of dissonance.

Drakengard 1,2 and 3 seek to establish stories that invert on themselves, a tone of seriousness, madness and apocalypse will be built up, only to be confused, made worse or relieved by characters with extremely humanistic or absurd flaws. Absurd, but believable as real archetypes, telling a terrible story, unraveled by terrible characters, believing to be as heroes.

The objective of the games is to question the player:

"Should you deserve a happy ending,
if you are oblivious to the weight of your actions?"
